# Paper Reading List

This list tracks papers that support the long-term direction of this repository: MPC, sampling-based MPC, vehicle dynamics, learned dynamics, and rapid adaptation.

The "Group / Lab" column means the research group, lab, or institution behind the paper. When a clear lab name is available, use the lab name rather than only the professor name.

| Group / Lab | Title | Venue / Year | arXiv | Short description |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Georgia Tech Autonomous Control and Decision Systems Lab | Information Theoretic Model Predictive Control: Theory and Applications to Autonomous Driving | IEEE Transactions on Robotics, 2018 | [arXiv:1707.02342](https://arxiv.org/abs/1707.02342) | Derives information-theoretic MPC and applies sampling-based control to aggressive autonomous driving. |
| Seoul National University DYROS Lab | Smooth Model Predictive Path Integral Control without Smoothing | IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters / IROS, 2022 | [arXiv:2112.09988](https://arxiv.org/abs/2112.09988) | Extends MPPI with input lifting and action costs to generate smoother controls without external smoothing filters. |
| BAIR-FAIR collaboration, UC Berkeley / CMU / Facebook AI Research | RMA: Rapid Motor Adaptation for Legged Robots | Robotics: Science and Systems, 2021 | [arXiv:2107.04034](https://arxiv.org/abs/2107.04034) | Introduces a base policy plus adaptation module for real-time sim-to-real locomotion adaptation. |
| UC Berkeley Hybrid Robotics / BAIR, with CMU | Adapting Rapid Motor Adaptation for Bipedal Robots | IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems, 2022 | [arXiv:2205.15299](https://arxiv.org/abs/2205.15299) | Extends RMA-style adaptation to Cassie biped walking with additional policy fine-tuning. |
| LeCAR Lab, Carnegie Mellon University | AnyCar to Anywhere: Learning Universal Dynamics Model for Agile and Adaptive Mobility | IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation, 2025 | [arXiv:2409.15783](https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.15783) | Learns a generalist vehicle dynamics model and combines it with sampling-based MPC for agile mobility. |
| LeCAR Lab, Carnegie Mellon University | Safe Deep Policy Adaptation | IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation, 2024 | [arXiv:2310.08602](https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.08602) | Combines adaptive policies, learned dynamics, and CBF-based safety filtering for safer real-world adaptation. |
